Even though humanity has never been more advanced than the current, there are many people who still sleep hungry, walk home in fear and others, suffer in their own form. I dream of a day where talented people can join me in this cause and contribute their skills towards helping others. One of my main goal in life is to establish a system or organization to do Pro Bono work in the technology space so that non-profit organizations can help others easily.
