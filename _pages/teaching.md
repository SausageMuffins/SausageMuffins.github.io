### Undergraduate Teaching Opportunities Programme (SUTD)
I was fortunate to take up the role of an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant during my time in Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD). Below are some of the modules that participated in as an undergraduate teaching assistant.

#### 10.014 Computational Thinking for Design 
SEP 2022 - DEC 2022
- Taught small groups (4-5 per group) of students in the 10.014 Computational Thinking for Design module.			   
- Graded home assignments and lab work (programming a Raspberry Pi microcontroller) and maintained student records.
- Assisted faculty with the preparation of 20 unique questions for upcoming classes that test students on their coding (Python) abilities - search algorithms, functions and object-oriented programming questions.


#### 10.013 Modelling and Analysis
SEP 2022 - DEC 2022
- Taught small groups (4-5 per group) of students in the 10.013 Modelling and Analysis module, focused on Calculus and Functions.
- Graded and home assignments to maintain student records.
- Assisted faculty and provided aid to students during tutorial session.
